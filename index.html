About Us

It is basically a Fitness Website for the fitness lovers who want to remain fit either by going Gym or staying at Home.Here we provide a platform to the users where they can view what is needed for remaining Fit.Basically,there are three different sections Like:"WorkOut","Diet Plan" and the "BMI Calculator" where they can visit and see what is needed for them.'WorkOut Section' consists of the details of different WorkOut categories we can do at Home,Like: Running,PushUp,Plank,Crunches, Mountain Climber and Squat.'Diet Plan Section' consists of the details of different Diet categories we can take while doing WorkOut at Home or at Gym,Like: Healthy Fruits,Chicken,Eggs,Milk,Dry Fruits,Rajma and through 'BMI Calculator Section' we get the Body Mass Index(BMI) from our Weight and Height.
