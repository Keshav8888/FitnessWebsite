About Us

WorkOut

Running

Running is basically done as: 1.Broaden your chest,keep it lifted as you draw your shoulders down and back. 2.Keep your hands loose,and use a relaxed arm swing.You should try to do at least 10 minutes of running everyday and 4 to 5 days a week.This will help you reduce your risk of death from Heart Attacks,Strokes,and other Diseases.

Push Up

Push Up is basically done as: 1.Get down on all fours,placing your hands slightly wider than your shoulders. 2.Straighten your arms and legs. 3.Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. 4.Pause,then push yourself back up. 5.Repeat.You should try to do 3 sets of 12 reps each day.This will help you Gain Muscle Strength.

Plank

Plank is basically done as: 1.Plant hands directly under shoulders like push-up. 2.Ground toes into the floor and stabilize your body.Your legs should be working. 3.Neutralize your neck and spine. 4.Hold the position for 20 seconds.You should try to do 3 sets of 30 seconds each day.This will help you reduce Belly Fat,Improves Balance,Posture.

Crunches

Crunches is basically done as: 1.Lie down on your back.Plant your feet on the floor.Bend your knees.Contract your abs and inhale. 2.Exhale and lift your upper body,keeping your head and neck relaxed. 3.Inhale and return to the starting position.You should try to do 3 sets of 12 to 15 reps.This will help you Better Cardiovascular Health,Weight Loss.

Mountain Climber

Mountain Climber is basically done as: 1.Get down on all fours,move your right thigh toward your chest and place your toes on the floor. 2.Straighten your left leg behind you,flex your foot,place it on the floor. 3.Switch legs without moving your arms. 4.Repeat.You should try to do 10 to 15 reps on each side,for 3 to 4 sets.This will help you Improve Mobolity,Support Heart Health.

Squat

Squat is basically done as: 1.Stand straight with feet hip-width apart. 2.Tighten your stomach muscles. 3.Lower down,as if sitting in an invisible chair. 4.Straighten your legs to lift back up. 5.Repeat the movement.You should try to do 10 to 20 times of 3 sets 3 times a week.This will help you Burn Calories and might help you Lose Weight.
